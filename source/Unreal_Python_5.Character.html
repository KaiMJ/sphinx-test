move_character_along_direction(direction, step_size=150)

Move the character along direction with one step. Note: If there’s an obstacle in the way, character will stop at the obstacle.

Parameters:

direction – 4 directions are supplied. “forward” , “backward”, “left” and “right”

represents the forward, backward, left and right direction of the character. :type step_size: :param step_size: Step size of this step. In default, it’s set as 150.

Returns:

Character’s final location as tuple(x, y, z)
